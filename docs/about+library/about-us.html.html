About Us

At Stranded on State our mission is to give the world a taste of college life at UW-Madison, and create funny, relateable content for every college student. We seek to avoid artificial depictions of our city and school, and to provide meaningful entertainment for students, by students. Below is a list of our contributors, and information about them. If you want to support us, like, share, and follow our social media accounts, and keep on being weird.
